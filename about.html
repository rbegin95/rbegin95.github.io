Hey there! My name is Richard, but most people know me as Chunk — the name behind Chunk's Detailing. I'm 30 years old and proudly based in the Niagara Region, where I’ve built my reputation for turning everyday vehicles into showroom-quality rides.

What started as a weekend passion quickly grew into something much more. With an eye for detail, a love for cars, and a dedication to customer satisfaction, I founded Chunk's Detailing to bring top-tier auto detailing to local drivers who care about how their vehicles look and feel.

At Chunk's Detailing, it’s not just about the shine — it’s about the trust, precision, and pride that goes into every car I touch.
